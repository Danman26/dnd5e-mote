# Grimbeard

**Grimbeard** runs the [Needledale](../../../societies/esterfell-accord/needledale.md) chapter of [Reynard's Den](../reynards-den.md).

## Information

- pronouns: she/her
- species: duergar
- languages: Common, Dwarvish

> [!TIP]
