**Krueger** heads the [Redgrave](../../../societies/esterfell-accord/redgrave.md) chapter of [Reynard's Den](../reynards-den.md).

## Information

- pronouns: he/him
- species: bugbear
- languages: Common, Goblin
- aliases: The Hare
